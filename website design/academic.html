Academics

The University offers Academic programmes through its faculties. The Academic programmes of the University are - Undergraduate Programme leading to B. Tech, B. Des.,B.A.(Hons), B.Sc.(Hons), B.Sc.(Data Sciences), B.Com.(Hons), BBA, BDS, B. Pharm., Pharm. D, BHM degree. Postgraduate Programme leading to M. Tech., M. Des., MBA, M.Sc., M.Com, Masters in Public Health, MDS, MHA, M. Pharm., MA(Public Policy) degree. Doctoral Programme leading to Ph.D. degree.
